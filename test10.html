Ps Pastor Rao, senior Pastor and overseer of God's Fire Fellowship Trust (GFFT), India. He was born in a non-believer family, and while pursuing truth he came to know about Jesus in the year 2000. His life was involved in all types of intoxicants but, after encountering with Jesus his life changed and he fully dedicated his life to Him. For 7 years he served God as a believer and finally in 2007 he began his service in a village church and continued there for next five years. Besides in 2008 by the grace of God he also started his service in Kanpur Nagar which endures till date. Presently Ps Pradeep Rao, is preaching the gospel and spreading the love of Jesus Christ to people.
